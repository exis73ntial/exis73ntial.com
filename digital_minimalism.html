home digital minimalism

Ideals

  1. No social media (YouTube, TikTok, anything Meta, Reddit, Twitter/X, LinkedIn)
    • Using sites for a specific purpose, such as looking something up without being logged in, is fine
  2. Minimize number of notifications wherever possible
  3. Fully close apps when not using for specific purpose (Discord...)
  4. Keep phone on gray-scale by default (disable and then re-enable when desired)
  5. Use pomodoro method with specific tasks for focused and intentional studying/working
  6. Read 30 minutes/day
